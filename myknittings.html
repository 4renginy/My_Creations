Pink Cardigan and Hat Set


This pink cardigan is done with Bernat Velvet yarn. This particular yarn feels like a silk when you knit it. I have knitted the cardigan as one piece starting from the neck. Starting stitch was garter stitch and continued with that stitch at the edges and bottom of the body. After finishing all I have done croche at the edges. The reason I do this becuase it prevens edges from sagging or expanding. Keeps it tight and aligned.

Blue and White Cardigan

This cardigan is one of my proud works. I have used tunisian knit stitch to make it and that was my first time using using this knitting style. Tunisian crochet is done by a long needle like knitting needles but the tip of it is like a croche hook so you can squize in a lot of stitces and knit is easily.

I have used once again Bernart Velvet Blue and White yarns to make this lovely cardigan, as usuall I have croched the edges to prevent stretching.

The nature of the knitting style it is very tick great for winter time yet it is silky soft becasue of the material. I enjoyed this cardigan very much.

This is a great sweater for little girls at the age 1-3 years old.
